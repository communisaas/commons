**Status:** Canonical architecture. Build Increment 1 (the Mantle frame + authoring-first home) is the immediate scope; the four workspace pages are a roadmap.
**Evolves:** [`ORG-REDESIGN-THESIS.md`](./ORG-REDESIGN-THESIS.md) — verification-first → **authoring-first, verification-as-watermark**.
**Cross-references:** [`VERIFICATION-LEGIBILITY.md`](./VERIFICATION-LEGIBILITY.md) (tiers/metrics are infrastructure, not headlines; staffer-legible packets), [`ORG-CAPABILITY-SCOPE.md`](./ORG-CAPABILITY-SCOPE.md) (shipped substrate vs. explicit stubs).

## Thesis

The org runs one loop:

```
INTENT → GROUND → AUTHOR → RESOLVE → SEND → AGGREGATE
```

The OS is the AI-native authoring-to-delivery instrument for that loop. Authoring is the strong center. Delivery is its consummation. Aggregation is its return.

**Verification is the watermark on every action** — an ambient signal-weight stamped onto what the org authors and sends, never the headline. This is the evolution of the prior thesis. `ORG-REDESIGN-THESIS.md` established "make CRM a consequence of verification, not verification a feature of CRM," and inverted the information hierarchy so that the proof packet became the figure and operational tools became the ground. That inversion was correct and shipped. It is now carried one step further: verification recedes from _figure_ to _watermark_, and **authoring** takes the center.

The thesis is therefore three-valued:

- **Authoring-first** — the action you author and send is the center of the OS.
- **Verification-as-watermark** — verification is ambient signal-weight on every action, legible but subordinate (consistent with `VERIFICATION-LEGIBILITY.md`: tiers and integrity metrics are infrastructure, not headlines).
- **Never CRM-first** — contact management remains a consequence, never the organizing principle.

The single most consequential structural move: **the Verification Packet moves out of the center and into Results as a showable artifact.** In the prior design the packet was the dashboard hero. Here, authoring is the hero, sending consummates it, and the packet is what comes back — the watermark made showable, the proof you ship and the proof you display, living where Results lives.

## Interaction Grammar

- **Compose** — primary verb. One gesture from anywhere. Opens the authoring loop.
- **Ground / Author / Resolve** — agentic sub-steps. Reasoning is _streamed_ — that streaming is the system's **presence**. Ground = source discovery, Author = artifact authoring, Resolve = audience/decision-maker resolution.
- **Send** — consummation. Per-channel. Produces a Results receipt. Public action drafts and email composer drafts are delivery-surface draft handoffs into existing delivery surfaces. The **CWC channel is marked not-yet-armed** until the congressional proof-delivery gate opens.

- **Spring only for coordination signals** — counts, signal-weight, accumulated state. Use the named `SPRINGS` configs; never invent spring parameters.

## Honest Hazards

- **Do not fork publishing.** `TemplateCreator` is not mounted at `/org/[slug]/compose` — that route does not exist. STUDIO may be org-scoped as the authoring surface, but publication still goes through the existing `/?create=true&resumeDraft=` contract. Synthesizing an org-scoped publish route breaks creation and fragments the mature flow.
- **Draft scoping on shared devices.** `templateDraftStore` scopes drafts by `ownerHash` (SHA-256 of userId). Pre-auth, `currentOwnerHash` is null and all drafts are visible; after login `setOwner()` must run or legacy drafts go invisible and cross-user collisions become possible. The Mantle must handle the guest→authenticated transition deliberately.
- **The watermark must not lie.** The org root no longer owns the first-read data surface; the layout feeds the Mantle and mounted Results space from verified-live slices. Do not reintroduce dashboard-local counters for `templates`, `emailReach`, `endorsedTemplates`, `followedReps`, `watchedBills`, or `legislativeAlerts` unless they are backed by real queries and cited in the surface.
- **Role is displayed, not enforced, at the layout level.** `membership.role` is surfaced (`+layout.server.ts:29-32`) but route gating lives in individual `+page.server.ts` files. The Mantle changes presentation only; it confers no new access. Any RBAC affordance in the Substrate band must not be mistaken for enforcement.
- **Latent verbs must stay visually held.** Workflow email, SMS dispatch, server-side email dispatch, call initiation, and CWC are not armed until their configured channel gates and runtime dependencies are clear. SMS dispatch requires `getTextDispatchReadiness`, a route-local client-decrypted E.164 batch, and `CP-sms-dispatch`; CWC specifically requires the congressional launch gate plus Convex-reported transport readiness, then each submission remains proof/template/witness/chamber bounded. Bounded workflow tag/branch/delay execution can be shown as armed only through `WORKFLOW_EXECUTION` plus the workflow effects gate. Client-direct merge personalization is armed only inside the browser-direct boundary; pages must still hold large/no-key/server paths and any channel without its required org key, dispatch claim, Lambda proxy, SES receipts, or cohort filter.
- **The capability map must be evidence-bound.** It may summarize cluster state, but its numbers must come from `OrgSpacesData`, configured channel gates, or adapter-backed task evidence, and every deferred claim must point at a concrete task/hypergraph unlock. Sent-email counts in the map cite the mounted Results slice (`OrgSpacesData.return.stats.sentEmails`) rather than a dashboard-era source label. Do not add aspirational metrics to make a capability card feel full.
- **Results' central artifact has gaps behind it.** Coalition stats (`networks.getStats`) are descriptive, not settlement-proof. The `accountabilityReceipts` writer is bounded to accepted, receipt-eligible proof email deliveries; it is not a general receipt substrate for SMS, webhooks, donations, or coalition artifacts. The on-chain anchor cron is ops-gated, and `RegistryMark` honestly reads "Sepolia testnet." Results must present the packet as the strong, real artifact it is (`computeVerificationPacketCached` is shipped and KV-cached) without overstating the settlement layer beneath it.
- **Mantle width and the content container.** Children render inside `<div class="p-6 md:p-8 max-w-5xl">` (`+layout.svelte:168`). A Mantle wider than `max-w-5xl` will be constrained unless it escapes the container with `w-full` and its own max-width.
- **This is an evolution, not a teardown.** `ORG-REDESIGN-THESIS.md` shipped a correct verification-first inversion across ~8,000 lines. Authoring-first re-centers the loop; it does not invalidate the prior work — the packet, the pipeline, the integrity assessment, and the perceptual-bridge design tests remain canonical. The hazard is treating this as a rewrite rather than a re-centering.
